Pool Services in Desert Mountain, Arizona

Uncompromising pool care for Scottsdale's most exclusive communities

Weekly Pool Cleaning & Maintenance

Our most popular service keeps your Desert Mountain pool in perfect condition all year long. Each weekly visit includes skimming the surface, brushing walls and tile, vacuuming the floor, emptying baskets, testing and balancing water chemistry, and inspecting all equipment.

Arizona's intense sun and desert conditions mean your pool needs consistent, expert attention. Our technicians understand the specific challenges Desert Mountain pools face — from mineral buildup to rapid chlorine loss caused by UV exposure. We adjust our treatments seasonally to keep your water perfectly balanced.

What's included: Surface skimming, wall & tile brushing, floor vacuuming, basket cleaning, chemical testing & balancing, equipment inspection, filter pressure check.

Green-to-Clean Pool Recovery

Come back from a trip to find your pool has turned green? Don't panic. Our green-to-clean service will restore your pool to sparkling condition — usually within 48 to 72 hours. We've recovered hundreds of neglected pools across Desert Mountain and the surrounding communities.

Our process includes shock treatment, algae elimination, thorough brushing, filter cleaning, and complete water rebalancing. We'll also diagnose what caused the issue to prevent it from happening again.

Pool Equipment Repair & Installation

From variable-speed pumps to energy-efficient heaters, we service and install all major brands of pool equipment. Our technicians carry common parts on their trucks so many repairs can be completed on the first visit.

Equipment we service: Pumps (single, dual, and variable speed), DE & cartridge filters, gas and heat pump heaters, chlorinators, automation systems, pool lights, valves, and plumbing.

Filter Cleaning & Replacement

A clean filter is essential for clear water and efficient circulation. We provide thorough filter cleans for cartridge, DE, and sand filters. We recommend filter cleaning every 3-4 months in Desert Mountain due to the high dust and debris common in the desert environment.

Salt System Installation & Service

Salt chlorine generators are increasingly popular in Desert Mountain for their gentle, consistent chlorination. We install, maintain, and repair all major salt system brands including Pentair, Hayward, and Jandy. Our team will help you determine if a salt system is right for your pool.

Pool Automation & Smart Controls

Control your pool from your smartphone. We install and program automation systems that let you manage your pump, heater, lights, and water features from anywhere. Popular systems include Pentair IntelliCenter, Hayward OmniLogic, and Jandy iAqualink.
