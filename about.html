About Desert Mountain Pool Cleaning

Your neighbors in Desert Mountain, keeping pools pristine since 2010

Our Story

Christopher Lane founded Desert Mountain Pool Cleaning in 2010 after a career managing private club facilities across Arizona and California. Having overseen pool operations at several exclusive country clubs, he recognized that homeowners in communities like Desert Mountain deserved the same caliber of service — precise, discreet, and uncompromising.

From a single truck serving Desert Mountain residents, Christopher built a team of elite pool technicians who specialize in high-end residential pools. Today, the company maintains over 170 pools across Desert Mountain, Carefree, and the finest enclaves of North Scottsdale.

Why Desert Mountain Trusts Us

Desert Mountain homes represent the pinnacle of Scottsdale living. Your pool should reflect that standard. We deliver private club-level pool care with the personal attention that only a boutique service company can provide.

Our Commitment

We understand that your pool is a significant investment and a centerpiece of outdoor living here in the Valley. Whether you're in a gated community or a custom home, we treat every pool as if it were our own.

  • Private club-level service for your personal pool
  • 15+ years specializing in luxury desert pools
  • Licensed, bonded & fully insured
  • Gate codes, security protocols — we know Desert Mountain
  • Discreet, punctual, and meticulous every visit
  • Eco-friendly chemical options available

Service Guarantee

If you're not completely satisfied with any service visit, let us know within 24 hours and we'll come back to make it right — at no additional charge. Period.
